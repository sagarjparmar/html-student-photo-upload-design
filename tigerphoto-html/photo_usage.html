Every entering first-year student is required to complete this form and submit a recent photograph by June 30, 2019.
Please read the following instructions carefully. These instructions must be followed to ensure accuracy in identifying you as a member of the Princeton community.
For questions regarding this form, please contact: https://oit.princeton.edu/

Student Photo Usage


The picture you submit will be used for four purposes:

University Identification Card

The TigerCard Office uses your photo to produce your University ID card. The TigerCard identifies you as a Princeton University student. It serves as your meal card, allowing access to your Dining Services meal plan; as a debit/credit card for campus purchases; and as an electronic door access card that enables you to gain entrance to campus dormitories, academic buildings, the library, gymnasium, athletic events, and other University activities.


University Student Records System

Your photo will be loaded into our PeopleSoft Student Record system where it will be viewed by your professors, your academic adviser, your dean, as well as limited University staff who have security authorization to view your internal academic record.


University Photo Directory

The Princeton University Photo Directory is produced in two ways: a print version and an online version. The print version is a historical publication of your class and will serve as an invaluable reference book both during and after college. It includes pictures, hometowns, and birthdays of your classmates, as well as helpful facts and information about Princeton and the campus community. The online version offers information included in the print version as well as many other options, such as advanced search features, customized profiles, student organization message boards, personal pictures, e-postcards, e-journal, calendar, meal exchanges, and more.


Residential College Directories

Each residential college will use your picture to produce a college photo directory. You have the right to withhold publication of your photograph in University publications by notifying the Office of the Registrar of this preference within 60 days of Academic Year Sign-In.
