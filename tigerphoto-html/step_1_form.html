Every entering first-year student is required to complete this form and submit a recent photograph by June 30, 2019.
Please read the following instructions carefully. These instructions must be followed to ensure accuracy in identifying you as a member of the Princeton community.
For questions regarding this form, please contact: https://oit.princeton.edu/

Photo Upload


This application allows you to upload a picture of yourself to be used electronically for purpose listed on the preceding page.

Your photo must meet the following requirements and subject to approval.

  • Use Only JPEG(.jpg) images
  • Image must be at least 15 kilobyte(KB), but no more than 1- megabytes(MB)
  • Use White, plain and indoor background
  • Photo must be in color
  • Photo must be in focus using strong lighting
  • Photo must be a head shot showing your entire face and head, facing forward with eyes open
  • No hats, headbands,sunglasses or other objects close to your face
  • You must be the only person in photo
